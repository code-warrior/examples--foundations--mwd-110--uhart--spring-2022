Choose this form to switch your major, add a major or a minor, or change/add a concentration or emphasis to your current program.

Note: An interactive layer has been added to this form in order to create a more cohesive and intuitive experience for all users requiring the use of this document. Consequently, this form differs from the official university form. You’re strongly advised to use this form over The University’s version.

  • Requires Adobe Acrobat Reader. Do not use macOS’s Preview or other third-party tool to fill in this document.
  • Requires one or more digital signatures
